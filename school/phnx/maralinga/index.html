Maralinga Atomic Bomb Testing

By Vijay, Cody & Ben

Who Was Responsible?

British service man/government were responsible for the tragic event at Maralinga which took the lives of many innocent aboriginal people and left a sacred land in ruin.


What Did the Bomb do to Maralinga?

Corrupted Maralinga with radiation poisoning which killed off animals, plantlife and even people. It also (of course) destroyed most of Maralinga.


When Were The Bombs Used?

The first bomb was used in October 1953 and the last one was detonated in 1963.


When Might Maralinga be Cleared of Radiation?

The particular type of material used at Maralinga was plutonium which the scientists thought would only last 50 years, but plutonium can have a nuclear fallour of 24,000 years meaning it is going to be a long time until it is safe to go to Maralinga
