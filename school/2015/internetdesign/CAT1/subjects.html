The Great Outdoors

The subject name basically explains it all. In this class we often will ride bikes around the streets, go swimming & surfing at the beach, & even sometimes we will go canoing down some rivers. I like this class becuase it can get me away from the school and I can go for rides & have fun with my other freinds in that class.

Robotics

Graphic Design

Graphic Design is a class where you explore the all the possibilities that you can do with grahpics, & design. We learn that simple logos such as the Twitter logo, aren't so simple. There are multiple hidden meanings behind each and every logo, if they're designed well.
