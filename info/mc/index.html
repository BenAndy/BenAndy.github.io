Cubed! Con | Livestream Moderator
Cubed! Con is basically Minecon, in game in Minecraft - It is officially the largest in-game convention. As a livestream moderator I watch over the chat, answer questions anyone has to ask and keep the rules in line. When there is not a current livestream I can log onto the Cubed! servers and moderate in-game there.

The Chunk Network | Moderator
The Chunk Network is a small mini-game network. Averaging about 400-600 players, I have to deal with them all. My job as a moderator on The Chunk is to keep the rules in line & keep the community happy with what powers I have access too. I can kick/mute/ban players who decide to break the rules.
