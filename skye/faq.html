Frequently Asked Questions
Here is a list to the most commonly asked questions, and their answers. If you don't find a question or an answer you're looking for, feel free to contact us.



I like Skye, but I use FireFox. Can I still use Skye New Tab?
At the moment, no. Skye New Tab is currently only a Google Chrome extension, but we plan to make it available to other browsers in the future.
I've found a bug, what do I do?
Report all issues, bugs & glitches HERE. You will need a GitHub account to report issues, bugs & glitches.
I have an idea for Skye New Tab, where can I present this idea to you?
You can present ideas and suggestions for Skye New Tab at the same place your report issues, bugs & glitches. Do so HERE. You will need a GitHub account to suggestion ideas and features.
