Hey there, please know that Skye New Tab is a Google Crhome extension and will not work on other browsers. If you wish to use Skye New Tab please download and install Google Chrome. Then you can download the plugin by pressing the "INSTALL NOW" button.





Replace your boring, default new tab page with a more modern and slick looking page! This page features the time, date, date alerts (for dates such as Christmas and New years), multiple background images - with a grayscale background option, social media icons, inspirational quotes, a timer, social media links and keyboard shortcuts! Background images and quotes are updated every ten days.

Everytime you open your new tab page, you will quickly be suprised with a modern new tab page. Each time you load the page, a random background image will be selected from a pool of wonderful photos provided by unsplash.com! The time, date, and an inspirational quote will also be shown to you - to help you keep track of the time, and keep you happy throughout the day.
