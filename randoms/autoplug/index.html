What can AutoPlug do?
- Automatically 'Woot' a new song
- Automatically join the Dj Wait List

What is AutoPlug?
AutoPlug changes the way that plug.dj works. It can automatally do tasks for you and level you up faster! You will be the Dj in bigger communities and pressing the 'Woot' button will no longer be your problem.
